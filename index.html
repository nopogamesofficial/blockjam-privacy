This Privacy Policy explains how Nopo ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Block Jam: Picture Puzzle (the "Game") on iOS and Android. By downloading or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require an account, and we do not knowingly collect your name, email address, or contact details inside the Game. The Game and the analytics services it uses may automatically collect:

  • Device & technical data: device model, operating system and version, language, region/country, app version, and unique app/device and advertising identifiers used by analytics and advertising.
  • Usage & gameplay data: levels played, progress, session length, in-game events, and feature interactions.
  • Crash & diagnostic data: crash logs, stack traces, and device state at the time of a crash, used to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Approximate location: coarse country/region inferred from your IP address by analytics services. We do not collect precise GPS location.

Your game progress and settings (such as level progress, lives, and your sound, haptic, and notification preferences) are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Game and its content.
  • To understand how players use the Game (analytics) and to balance difficulty and features.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix technical problems.
  • To display advertising and measure its performance.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse, and to comply with legal obligations.

To serve and measure ads, AdMob and its advertising partners may collect and use your device advertising identifier (Advertising ID on Android / IDFA on iOS), IP-based approximate location, and ad-interaction data. This data may be shared with Google and its advertising partners to display and measure advertising.

Where required by law (for example, under GDPR/ePrivacy in the EEA and UK, or Apple's App Tracking Transparency on iOS), we ask for your consent before using your advertising identifier for personalized ads, and you can choose non-personalized ads. You can also reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings.
